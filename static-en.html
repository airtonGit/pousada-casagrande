ABOUT US

Casagrande Hotel in Torres, Rio Grande do Sul

Celebrating 10 years in the most beautiful beach in southern Brazil, Casagrande Hotel shows a pattern of commitment to its guests which begins following the great care with comfort in all areas, like at reception, table games and TV room, pool and a delicious breakfast. All this to make you feel always happy to make you enjoy the well-deserved holiday moments intensely. Our satisfaction is to see you back!
